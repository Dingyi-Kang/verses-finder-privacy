Support — Verse Seek

Verse Seek helps you find and read the Bible verses cited in study material. Photograph a page, import an image, or type references, and the app looks up each verse and shows the full text in one list.

Getting started

  • Tap the + button and choose Camera, Photos, or Type.
  • For a quick test, choose Type and enter something like John 3:16; Eph. 4:4-6.
  • Tap a verse to open more context; use the heart to save it to Favorites.

Frequently asked questions

The app didn’t find a reference on my page.
Text recognition works best on clear, well-lit, printed pages. Try retaking the photo straight-on with good lighting, or type the reference directly. Handwriting and very stylized fonts may not be recognized.

A verse won’t load.
Verse text is fetched online, so an internet connection is required. If a lookup fails, check your connection and tap refresh.

Which translations are available?
The Recovery Version, plus the public-domain King James Version, World English Bible, American Standard Version, and Bible in Basic English. Choose your translation in Settings.

Are my photos uploaded?
No. Images are read on your device and are never uploaded. Only the verse reference (e.g. “John 3:16”) is sent, to fetch the text. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Is it free?
Yes — completely free, with nothing gated or locked.
